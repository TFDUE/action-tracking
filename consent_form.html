Information for participants

This information sheet outlines the purpose of the data collection and provides a description of your involvement and rights as a participant, if you agree to take part.

What is the research about?

With this extension we aim to gain information about different Jupyter notebook usage in online learning environments. Our goal is to gain insights with respect to intelligent learning systems. This extension is part of a Bachelor thesis for the University of Duisburg-Essen.

Do I have to take part?

It is up to you to decide whether or not to take part. You do not have to take part if you do not want to. If you do decide to take part you can give your consent by checking the box in the extension menu.

3. What will my involvement be?

During active tracking your interactions within the notebooks will be registered, saved and sent to a server with the current notebook you're working on. The recorded interactions contain the time spent working on the notebook, the amounts of clicks ,created and deleted cells, copy and paste commands, cell executions, executions errors, key presses, mouse movement and scrolling.

4. How do I withdraw from the study?

You can withdraw from the data collection at any point without having to give a reason by unchecking the consent box, disabling or uninstalling the extension. Withdrawing from the data collection will have no effect on you. If you wish that the already collected data is not used you can contact the Principal Investigator Irene-Angelica.

5. What will my information be used for?

We will use the collected information for the purposes of the research into Intelligent Online Learning Environments

6. Will my taking part and my data be kept confidential? Will it be anonymised?

The records from this study will be kept confidential. Only the administrators will have access to the material generated from this workshop. Your data will be anonymised. Your name will not be used in any reports or publications. All digital files, transcripts and summaries will be given codes and stored separately from any names or other direct identification of participants. Any hard copies of research information will be kept in locked files at all times. Original data will be destroyed after it has served its initial purpose.

7. What if I have a question or complaint?

If you have any questions regarding this study please contact the Principal Investigator, Irene-Angelica Chounta via irene-angelica.chounta@uni-due.de
